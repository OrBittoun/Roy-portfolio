Pizza Margherita

Ingredients:
1 pizza dough
4 tbsp tomato sauce
125g fresh mozzarella, sliced
6-8 fresh basil leaves
1 tbsp olive oil
Sea salt to taste

Instructions:
1. Preheat your oven to 250°C or the highest possible.
2. Roll out the dough into a thin round on a baking sheet or pizza stone.
3. Spread a thin layer of tomato sauce evenly over the dough.
4. Arrange the mozzarella slices across the top, leaving small gaps.
5. Add fresh basil leaves on top (or after baking, for a fresher flavor).
6. Drizzle with olive oil and sprinkle with a pinch of sea salt.
7. Bake for 8-10 minutes, until the crust is golden and cheese is bubbling.

Pasta Carbonara

Ingredients:
200g spaghetti
100g pancetta or guanciale, diced
2 large eggs
50g grated pecorino romano cheese
Freshly ground black pepper
Salt (for pasta water)

Instructions:
1. Boil a pot of salted water and cook the spaghetti until al dente (about 8-9 minutes).
2. Fry the pancetta in a hot pan (no oil needed) until crispy. Remove from heat.
3. In a bowl, whisk together the eggs, pecorino cheese, and generous black pepper.
4. Drain the pasta and reserve a bit of the cooking water (about 1/4 cup).
5. Quickly mix the hot pasta with the egg mixture, stirring fast to avoid scrambling the eggs.
6. Add the pancetta and a splash of reserved pasta water to create a creamy texture.
7. Serve immediately, topped with extra pecorino and black pepper.

Lasagna

Ingredients:
9 lasagna noodles
400g ground beef
2 cups tomato sauce (or homemade meat sauce)
2 cups béchamel sauce
200g shredded mozzarella cheese
Salt and black pepper
Olive oil

Instructions:
1. Preheat your oven to 180°C.
2. Cook the lasagna noodles in salted water until al dente. Drain and set aside.
3. In a pan, heat olive oil and brown the ground beef. Season with salt and pepper.
4. Add tomato sauce to the meat and let it simmer for 10 minutes.
5. In a baking dish, spread a thin layer of meat sauce at the bottom.
6. Add a layer of noodles, then meat sauce, béchamel, and mozzarella. Repeat 2-3 times.
7. Finish with a layer of béchamel and a generous amount of mozzarella on top.
8. Bake for 35-40 minutes until golden and bubbling. Let rest 10 minutes before slicing.

Tiramisu

Ingredients:
200g ladyfingers (savoiardi)
250g mascarpone cheese
2 large eggs (separated)
60g sugar
200ml strong espresso (cooled)
Unsweetened cocoa powder (for dusting)

Instructions:
1. Separate the eggs. Whisk the yolks with sugar until pale and thick.
2. Add mascarpone to the yolk mixture and blend until smooth.
3. In a separate bowl, beat egg whites until stiff peaks form. Gently fold into the mascarpone mix.
4. Dip each ladyfinger briefly into the espresso.
5. Layer dipped ladyfingers at the base of a dish, then spread mascarpone mixture on top.
6. Repeat with another layer of soaked ladyfingers and finish with mascarpone.
7. Chill in the fridge for at least 4 hours. Dust with cocoa before serving.
